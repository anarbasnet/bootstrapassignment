Sports CLub

Club Oasis, an urban spa that is regarded as one of the best in Kathmandu, covers 1,400 square metres of the hotel and is located at S level, overlooking the Japanese garden. The spa offers a tranquil retreat for the senses and is a true experience of rejuvenation and relaxation for the body, mind, and soul. There are four treatment rooms and six treatment beds, and all products are organic.
